Who I Am

A little about
me

Hi, I'm Payton! I'm a licensed cosmetologist and Marketing Management student at William Paterson University, with a Psychology minor. My world lives at the intersection of beauty and business.

I specialize in cuts, coloring, and styling at V'va Hair Salon, where I build real relationships with clients and promote salon products. I'm also a barista at PJ's Coffee and a catering server at Cedar Crest Village — I thrive in fast-paced, people-first environments.

My goal is to bring a fresh, beauty-industry perspective to marketing — because I've lived both sides of the chair.
